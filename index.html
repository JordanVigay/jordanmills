Jordan Mills has been writing and performing his own music for the past ten years as well as performing a wide range of popular covers.

With a sound rooted in indie-pop and folk, Jordan's music often draws from personal stories and relatable experiences.

He has already released two albums and is gearing up for the release of his third.
