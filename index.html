Jordan Mills

Portsmouth based singer/songwriter

I've been writing and performing my own music for the last ten years. I also enjoy playing a wide range of covers, and have honed my skills busking on the streets of Portsmouth. I can tailor the style of music and song choice to suit most atmospheres, and have been booked to play at restaurants, weddings and other events.
